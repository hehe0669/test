Pascal's Triangle creater

Created by Bill Cao

Note: the algorithm limit is 171.



OPTIONAL


Note: the input can contain lowercased letter(a to z), number(0 to 9), and minus sign before the letter or number. The two term can't be the same.






Use right and left arrow to move the Pascal Triangle when it clipped out of the screen.

Press Tab to jump to the input box right away.

Press Enter to create the triangle.

Hover over each box to find its row and combination expression.
